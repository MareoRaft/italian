words for handle
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

il manico -- a handle with no moving parts, like on a garden tool

la maniglia -- a handle with moving parts, like a door handle or a car door handle

la manovella -- the handle of a crank or to operate a mechanism, like the handle to start a motor

il manubrio -- handle bars, the handle of weights, barbell
