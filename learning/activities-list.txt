Activities for learning italian
=================================


reading
-----------------
  * fairy tales
    - favole al telefono
  * books
    - cavaliere inesistente
    - Il Barone Rampante
    - L'amica geniale
  * newspapers




listening
-------------------
  * podcasts, such as Podcast Italiano
  * podcast, Duolingo
  * songs





writing
--------------------
  * write your own fairy tale
  * write your own short story
  * write a summary of a book
  * have a conversation and simultaneously write down what you are saying on Google Docs.  Post a picture into the doc and then talk about it.





watching
-------------------
  * watch shows dubbed into Italian, such as "The Crown" (dubbed shows have excellent grammar and pronunciation)
  * Learn Italian with Lucrezia channel on youtube
  * Un Italiano Vero channel on youtube
  * watch italian television series, such as L'amica geniale





grammar
------------------
  * textbook
  * worksheets from my italian teacher on Google Docs
  * fill in the blank exercises
    - http://www.impariamoitaliano.com/




vocabulary
-----------------
  * apps, such as Tiny Flash
  * Anki
  * word games
    - https://www.giochi123.net/gioco/microsoft-ultimate-word-games
  * board games
    - Taboo (but speak in italian)
    - sherades




conversing
-----------------
  * talk to a teacher from italki.com
  * debates, pick a topic and argue both sides
  * conversation starters
    - https://psycatgames.com/it/magazine/conversation-starters/questions-to-get-to-know-someone/
