"preposizioni"
--------------
situation:
  * departing for a place (partire)
  * passing by a place (passare)
-> per

situation:
  * staying at a place (abitare, vivere, stare, restare, rimanere)
  * going to a place (andare, viaggiare, tornare, correre)
large islands, regions, and larger -> in
small islands, towns -> a
generic places ending in ria, cia, teca -> in
chiesa, piscina, palestra, ufficcio -> in
other generic places -> a
person (nonna, dentista) -> da

situation:
  * coming from a place (venire, tornare...)
-> da




prepositions of time:
past to now -> da
now to future -> a
past -> per
future -> per
future moment -> tra




"loro" -> li
"a loro" -> gli
"di loro" -> ne
"da loro" -> ne
"con loro" -> con loro
per loro -> per loro
(these can't use a pronoun)



for the formal/educato, when doing a command, the mi comes still before:
"mi scusi"
informal
"scusami"
because the "scusi" here is congiuntivo/subjunction.  Congiuntivo is used for commands.

past situations --> past tenses
-------------------------------
(priority order)
habitual --> imperfetto
two things going on simultaneously --> imperfetto
one thing going on and other inserts --> imperfetto + passato prossimo
feelings / state of mind --> both, lean towards imperfetto
one time thing(s) --> passato prossimo
repeated in non habitual way --> passato prossimo
two things in succession --> passato prossimo
