I pezzi di scacchi
===================

  * re

  * donna -> means "woman". the queen.

  * alfiere -> from arab "al fil" meaning "elephant", since the piece was originally depicted as an elephant.  "alfiere" in italian means portabandiera.  The italian word for bishop is "vescovo".

  * cavallo -> means "horse". the knight.

  * torre -> means "tower". the rook.

  * pedone -> from the latin "pedonem" which means "foot soldier".
