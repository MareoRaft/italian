"ed": Do I say "i pro e i contro" or "i pro ed i contro"?
  * Definitive rule: for the words "e", "a", "o", iff next word begins with the same letter, use a "d".

  * note: people have begun to use the "d" when the next word begins with ANY vowel, even though this is not the official rule

Q
Are there situations where a sentence *begins* with "che", but CANNOT use "cosa" instead?
A
Che cibo vuoi?  Cosa cibo vuoi?
--> Francesca says "cosa cibo vuoi" is not correct.

"cosa" is the THING.  Therefore, if the THING exists in the sentence, you CANNOT use cosa.

For example,

Cosa maglia ti piace?
Cosa studente è il tuo preferito?
Cosa truffa hai inventato oggi?

Are all WRONG, because "maglia", "studente", and "truffa" are the THING.
