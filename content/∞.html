Stuff about things

``Determiners'' just means adjectives that have to do with amountsish. The ENDINGS of the adjectives must agree with the noun plurality and gender, except for qualunque and qualsiasi.

A little to a lot

nessuno — none(not one)
niente — none
qualunque — any(qualunque and qualsiasi are interchangeable)
qualsiasi — any
poco — little
molti — many
parecchie — many
tutto — everything

Miscellaneous

questi — these
quelli — those
altro — other
diversi — diverse
