Stuff about things

``Determiners'' just means adjectives that have to do with amountsish. The ENDINGS of the adjectives must agree with the noun plurality and gender, except for qualunque and qualsiasi. A little to a lot ------------------ nessuno - none (``not one'') niente - none qualunque - any qualsiasi - any poco - little alcuni - some molti - many parecchie - many tutto - everything Note: qualunque and qualsiasi are interchangeable..
